Biography [CV]

I am currently a final-year Ph.D student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), supervised by Prof. Jiaya Jia . Before that, I received the Bachelor's Degree at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2020.

My current research interest includes primarily in Multi-modal LLM, 3D Point Cloud / 2D Image Scene Understanding.
